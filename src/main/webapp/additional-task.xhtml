(Verse 1)
Beneath the willow's tender shade, the duck begins to play,
Quacking softly by the pond, a melody so gay.
Feathers shimmer in the sun, reflections in the muck,
Nature's poet, wading through, the tales of a wise duck.

(Chorus)
Duck, duck, in the morning light,
Duck, duck, dancing with delight.
Quack, quack, a rhythm in the reeds,
Duck, duck, fulfilling nature's needs.
